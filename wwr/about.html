History

Our company started with a passion for adventure and a love for the outdoors. Founded in 2005, we set out to provide thrilling, safe, and unforgettable whitewater rafting experiences. What began as a small team of rafting enthusiasts has grown into a trusted name in adventure tourism. Over the years, we have guided thousands of adventurers through scenic rivers, exhilarating rapids, and breathtaking canyons, always prioritizing safety, teamwork, and environmental responsibility. Whether you’re seeking a peaceful float or an adrenaline-pumping ride, Whitewater Rafting Co. is here to make your experience one to remember!

We have been providing thrilling rafting experiences since 2005.
